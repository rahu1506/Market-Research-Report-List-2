<p><p>MDI Prepolymers are used in various industries as raw materials for producing polyurethane foams, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. There are two main types of MDI Prepolymers: MDI (Polyether) and MDI (Polyester). MDI (Polyether) is a type of MDI Prepolymer where the polyether polyols are used as the main polyol component, resulting in flexible and elastic products. On the other hand, MDI (Polyester) utilizes polyester polyols, which make the final products more rigid and durable. The choice between these two types depends on the specific application requirements and desired properties of the end product.</p></p>
