<p><p>The hunting crossbow market can be divided into two main types: compound crossbows and recurve crossbows. Compound crossbows are more technologically advanced, featuring pulleys and cables that help generate more power and speed. They are often smaller in size and provide better accuracy. Recurve crossbows, on the other hand, are simpler in design with limbs that curve away from the shooter. They are known for their reliability, durability, and ease of maintenance. Ultimately, the choice between compound and recurve crossbows depends on the hunter's preferences and requirements.</p></p>
