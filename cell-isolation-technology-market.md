<p><p>Cell isolation technology is a crucial process in various research areas, such as cancer and stem cell research. There are three main types of cell isolation technology: centrifugation, flow cytometry, and cell electrophoresis. Centrifugation uses high-speed rotation to separate cells based on their size and density. Flow cytometry separates cells using laser-based technology, analyzing their unique properties, such as size, shape, and fluorescence. Cell electrophoresis separates cells based on their electrophoretic mobility, or movement in an electric field. These technologies help researchers study and isolate specific types of cells for further analysis and experimentation.</p></p>
