<p><p>Fiber Adapter Market Types such as FC, SC, ST, and LC are connectors used in fiber-optic communication systems. FC (Ferrule Connector) features a threaded connection and is commonly used in industrial applications. SC (Subscriber Connector) has a push-pull locking mechanism and is popular in telecommunications. ST (Straight Tip) connectors are used in network patch panels and multimedia applications. LC (Lucent Connector) offers a smaller form factor, making it suitable for high-density fiber-optic connections in data centers. These market types differ in their construction and application areas within the fiber-optic industry.</p></p>
