<p><p>Cartridge microfiltration is a market that offers various types of filters for different applications. PP (polypropylene) type filters are commonly used for water filtration due to their high chemical compatibility. PES (polyethersulfone) type filters are suitable for liquid sterilization applications in the pharmaceutical and food industries. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) type filters are known for their excellent chemical resistance and are used in aggressive chemical filtration. The "Other" market comprises various filter types tailored to specific industries, such as nylon filters for inkjet printing or cellulose acetate filters for wine production.</p></p>
