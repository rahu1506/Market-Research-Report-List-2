<p><p>Image tagging and annotation services market consists of various types, including image classification, object recognition, and boundary recognition. Image classification involves categorizing images based on their content, allowing for easy organization and retrieval. Object recognition is the process of identifying specific objects within an image, enabling applications such as facial recognition or tracking. Boundary recognition involves identifying and outlining the boundaries of objects in images, useful for applications like image segmentation or measuring objects. These different market types provide essential services for various industries, such as e-commerce, healthcare, or autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
