<p><p>Electronic signature verification is a rapidly growing market that can be categorized into two types: static signature verification and dynamic signature verification. Static signature verification involves the analysis of a static image of the signature to determine its authenticity. On the other hand, dynamic signature verification captures the dynamic characteristics of a signature, such as speed, pressure, and trajectory, to verify its legitimacy. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring secure and legal transactions by confirming the validity of electronic signatures.</p></p>
