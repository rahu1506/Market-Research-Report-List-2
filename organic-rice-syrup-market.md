<p><p>The organic rice syrup market is primarily divided into two types: brown rice and white rice. Brown rice syrup is derived from whole grain brown rice and contains more nutrients and fiber compared to white rice syrup. It has a nuttier flavor and is generally considered healthier. On the other hand, white rice syrup is made from polished white rice and has a milder taste. While both types are used as sweeteners in various food products, brown rice syrup is often preferred by health-conscious consumers due to its nutritional benefits.</p></p>
