<p><strong>The Meat Fat Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Meat Fat Analyzer market includes two major types of technologies: DEXA Technology and NIR Technology. DEXA Technology is a method that uses Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry to accurately measure the fat content in meat samples. It provides precise results by measuring the absorption of X-rays at different energy levels. On the other hand, NIR Technology utilizes Near-Infrared Spectroscopy to determine the fat composition in meat. This non-destructive method analyzes the interaction between the meat sample and NIR light to identify its fat content.</p></p>
