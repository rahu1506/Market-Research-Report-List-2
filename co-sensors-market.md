<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Portable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CO sensors are devices used to detect the presence of carbon monoxide gas. The market for CO sensors can be categorized into two types: fixed and portable. Fixed CO sensors are installed in a specific location, such as homes or offices, and constantly monitor the air for carbon monoxide. They provide round-the-clock protection and are commonly used in buildings where a continuous monitoring solution is required. Portable CO sensors, on the other hand, are portable devices that can be carried around and used in various locations. These sensors are suitable for personal use or for situations where mobility is necessary, like checking for carbon monoxide in different areas or vehicles.</p></p>
