<p><p>Concrete sand heaters are used in construction projects to heat the sand before mixing it with other materials. There are two main types of concrete sand heaters: stationary and portable. Stationary sand heaters are permanently installed at a construction site and heat large quantities of sand continuously. They are ideal for long-term projects. On the other hand, portable sand heaters are designed to be easily transported from one location to another. They are more versatile and suitable for smaller construction projects or those that require sand heating in multiple locations.</p></p>
