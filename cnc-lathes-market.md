<p><p>CNC lathes are machines used for turning operations in manufacturing processes. The market for CNC lathes can be categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical CNC lathes have a vertical spindle axis and are commonly used for larger and heavier workpieces. They provide easy accessibility to the workpiece and are suitable for turning complicated shapes. On the other hand, horizontal CNC lathes have a horizontal spindle axis and are typically used for smaller workpieces and high-precision turning operations. They offer enhanced stability and accuracy due to their horizontal orientation.</p></p>
