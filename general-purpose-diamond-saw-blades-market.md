<p><strong>The General Purpose Diamond Saw Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintering</li><li>High-Frequency Welding</li><li>Laser Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General Purpose Diamond Saw Blades are available in three types: Sintering, High-Frequency Welding, and Laser Welding. Sintering blades are made by blending diamond grit with metal powders and heating in a furnace, offering good performance at a lower cost. High-Frequency Welding blades use a high-frequency heating process to bond the diamond segments to the blade core, providing a balance of performance and durability. Laser Welding blades use a laser to join the segments to the core, offering high precision and long-lasting reliability.</p></p>
