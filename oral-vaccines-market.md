<p><p>Oral vaccines are a type of vaccine that is administered through the mouth rather than through injections. They are designed to induce an immune response in the body by delivering a weakened or killed form of the target pathogen to the gastrointestinal tract. This method of administration is convenient, particularly for children, as it does not require the use of needles.</p><p>The oral vaccines market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of infectious diseases worldwide is driving the demand for effective vaccination strategies. Oral vaccines offer a convenient and cost-effective solution, making them attractive for mass immunization campaigns and in low-resource settings.</p><p>Moreover, the advancements in biotechnology have facilitated the development of more efficacious and stable oral vaccines. These advancements include innovations in recombinant DNA technology, use of novel adjuvants, and improved methods for vaccine stabilization. Additionally, the rising focus on preventive healthcare and the growing awareness about the benefits of vaccines are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the oral vaccines market is the development of oral vaccines for diseases that were previously only preventable through injectable vaccines. For instance, oral vaccines for diseases such as rotavirus and polio have gained significant popularity in recent years.</p><p>Furthermore, the ongoing research and development activities in the field of oral vaccines are aimed at expanding the range of diseases that can be prevented through this route of administration. This includes the development of oral vaccines for diseases such as tuberculosis, cholera, norovirus, and influenza.</p><p>Overall, the oral vaccines market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
