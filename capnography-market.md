<p><p>Capnography is a medical monitoring technique used to measure the level of carbon dioxide in a patient's breath. The Capnography market includes various types of devices and disposables. Main-Stream Capnographs are integrated into the patient's breathing circuit, directly measuring CO2 levels. Side-Stream Capnographs use a sampling tube to draw breath samples from the patient. Micro-Stream Capnographs use a small sample of patient breath to measure CO2 levels. Capnography Disposables consist of various accessories like nasal cannulas and filters that are used along with capnography devices for precise measurements.</p></p>
