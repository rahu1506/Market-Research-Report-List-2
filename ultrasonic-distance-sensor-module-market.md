<p><p>Ultrasonic distance sensor modules are primarily categorized into two types: analog type and digital type. The analog type market refers to the segment where sensor modules provide distance measurement as an analog output voltage or current. These modules are suitable when a continuous range of distance information is required. On the other hand, the digital type market involves modules that provide distance measurement as a digital output signal, typically in the form of a pulse signal. These modules are useful when discrete distance measurements are needed.</p></p>
