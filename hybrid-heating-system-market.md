Other types of hybrid heating systems may include combining traditional gas or oil heating systems with renewable energy sources like solar panels or biomass boilers. These systems offer homeowners flexibility and the ability to select the most suitable and cost-effective heating option based on their geography and energy requirements.</p></p>
