<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>In the 5G market, dielectric resonators are used for various applications. TEM mode refers to Transverse Electromagnetic mode, where both electric and magnetic fields are perpendicular to the direction of wave propagation within the dielectric resonator. TE mode stands for Transverse Electric mode, where the electric field is perpendicular to the direction of wave propagation. TM mode represents Transverse Magnetic mode, where the magnetic field is perpendicular to the direction of wave propagation. These different modes allow the dielectric resonators to efficiently handle and manipulate electromagnetic waves for enhanced 5G communication.</p></p>
