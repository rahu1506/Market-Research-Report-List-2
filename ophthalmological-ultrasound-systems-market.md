<p><p>Ophthalmological ultrasound systems are medical devices used for diagnosing eye disorders. There are two types of systems available in the market: fixed and mobile. Fixed systems are large and are typically found in hospital settings or specialized eye clinics, providing a permanent installation for high-quality imaging. On the other hand, mobile systems are portable and can be moved around to different locations, allowing for flexibility in providing eye examinations and diagnostics in various medical facilities or remote areas. Both types serve the purpose of non-invasively capturing images of the eye to aid in diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
