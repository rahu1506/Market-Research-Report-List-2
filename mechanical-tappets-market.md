<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical tappets are essential components in the valve mechanism of internal combustion engines. The market for mechanical tappets can be classified into two types: flat tappet and roller tappet. Flat tappets are flat-surfaced components that slide along the camshaft lobes, while roller tappets have a roller on the bottom that rolls along the camshaft, reducing friction and wear. Both types of tappets have specific advantages and are used in different engine designs, with flat tappets being more commonly found in older engines and roller tappets being preferred for modern high-performance engines.</p></p>
