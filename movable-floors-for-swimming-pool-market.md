<p><p>Movable floors for swimming pool come in two main types: Suspended Type and Floating Type. Suspended Type movable floors are supported by a structure below the pool, allowing them to be raised or lowered to adjust the depth of the pool. Floating Type movable floors use buoyant materials to adjust the pool depth. Both types offer versatility in pool design and functionality, allowing for customization of the pool depth to meet various needs, such as children's swimming lessons, aqua therapy, or recreational swimming.</p></p>
