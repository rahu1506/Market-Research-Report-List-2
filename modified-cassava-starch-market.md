The rising demand for convenience food products, including ready-to-eat meals and processed snacks, is one of the major factors driving market growth. Modified cassava starch is used as a thickening, stabilizing, and texturizing agent in these food products, enhancing their sensory attributes and shelf life.</p><p>The increasing adoption of modified cassava starch in the textile industry for sizing, finishing, and printing applications is also contributing to market growth. It offers improved binding properties, better fabric adhesion, and increased resistance to mechanical stress.
