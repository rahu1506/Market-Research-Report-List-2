<p><p>Polyurethane bars are divided into two market types: flexible urethane materials and rigid urethane materials. Flexible urethane materials are known for their elasticity and ability to withstand high levels of stress, making them suitable for applications where flexibility is crucial, such as automotive components and cushioning materials. Rigid urethane materials, on the other hand, are rigid and strong, making them ideal for applications that require stability and structural support, such as construction materials and industrial products. Both market types cater to different needs and industries, offering versatile solutions in various sectors.</p></p>
