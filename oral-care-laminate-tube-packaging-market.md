<p><p>The Oral Care Laminate Tube Packaging market consists of two main types - ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes are composed of multiple layers including aluminum, ensuring excellent protection against light, moisture, and oxygen. PBL tubes, on the other hand, are made of plastic layers, offering great flexibility and durability. Both ABL and PBL tubes are widely used in the oral care industry for packaging products such as toothpaste, mouthwash, and gels. These types of packaging materials help maintain the quality and extend the shelf life of oral care products.</p></p>
