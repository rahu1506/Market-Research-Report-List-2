<p><p>Hydrostatic transmission, also known as a hydrostatic drive system, is a type of transmission commonly used in machinery and vehicles. It uses hydraulic power to control the speed and direction of motion. Unlike traditional transmission systems that utilize gears and clutches, a hydrostatic transmission uses a hydraulic pump and motor to transfer power and control the movement of the vehicle or machinery.</p><p>Hydrostatic transmissions offer several advantages over conventional transmissions, such as smooth and precise control over speed and direction, improved fuel efficiency, and reduced mechanical complexity. They are widely used in various applications, including agricultural machinery, construction equipment, lawn and garden tractors, and industrial machines.</p><p>The hydrostatic transmission market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period. Manufacturers are focusing on developing compact and lightweight transmission systems with higher efficiency and improved performance.
