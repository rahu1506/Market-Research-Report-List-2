<p><p>Brain matrices are scaffolding tools used in neurobiology and neuroscience research to provide support and structure for brain tissues during experiments and analyses. These matrices are made by freeze-thawing animal brains to extract the extracellular matrix (ECM) and preserve the natural architecture of brain tissues. Brain matrices aid in maintaining the integrity and functionality of brain samples, allowing researchers to study various cellular and molecular processes.</p><p>The global brain matrices market is witnessing significant growth due to the rising demand for neurobiology and neuroscience research.
