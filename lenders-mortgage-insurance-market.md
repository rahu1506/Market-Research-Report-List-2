<p><p>Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI) is a type of insurance that protects lenders against default by borrowers with low down payments. There are four market types for LMI: Borrower-paid (BPMI), where the borrower pays the premium as part of their monthly mortgage payment; Lender-paid (LPMI), where the lender pays the premium but charges a higher interest rate; Single Premium, where the borrower makes a one-time payment for the insurance; and Split Premium, where the borrower pays a portion of the premium upfront and the rest is added to the monthly mortgage payment.</p></p>
