<p><p>Low Vision Aids market encompasses three main types of aids: optical aids, non-optical aids, and electronic aids. Non-optical aids include tools like large-print materials, tactile graphics, and writing guides, which provide assistance through modifications or adaptations. Electronic aids encompass devices like CCTVs, screen readers, and wearable gadgets, which use advanced technology to enhance vision and provide audio or visual assistance. These aids aim to improve the quality of life for individuals with low vision by increasing their visual independence and functionality.</p></p>
