Rolling Resistance systems measure the energy required for tire rolling on different surfaces, influencing fuel efficiency. Tread Wear systems evaluate tire durability and predict its lifespan by subjecting it to simulated road conditions. These types of tire testing systems are crucial for manufacturers and researchers to ensure the quality and safety of tires before their use in the market.</p></p>
