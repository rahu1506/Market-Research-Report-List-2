<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Crystal Pullers market comprises two main types of furnaces used for crystal growth: the Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ Method Furnace employs a process where a seed crystal is dipped into a melt, then slowly withdrawn while rotating to allow for the growth of a large, high-quality crystal. On the other hand, the FZ Method Furnace employs a high-energy heat source to melt and solidify a rod-shaped crystal continuously, resulting in crystals with superior purity and structural integrity.</p></p>
