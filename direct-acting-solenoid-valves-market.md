<p><strong>The Direct-acting Solenoid Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Direct-acting solenoid valves are categorized into four main types. The first type is the step-by-step direct-acting diaphragm structure, which operates by the upward or downward movement of a diaphragm to open or close the valve. The second type is the direct-acting piston structure, where the piston directly controls the valve opening and closing. The third type is the direct motion diaphragm structure, which features a diaphragm directly linked to the valve disc for controlling flow. Lastly, the step-by-step piston structure utilizes a piston to achieve stepwise operation of the valve. These different types cater to various applications and preferences within the solenoid valve market.</p></p>
