<p><p>The Healthcare Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) market consists of two main types: cloud-based PACS and on-premise PACS. Cloud-based PACS involves storing and accessing medical images and data on remote servers, allowing for easy accessibility and sharing from any location. On the other hand, on-premise PACS involves locally storing and managing medical data within the healthcare facility. Both options have their own advantages and considerations, such as cost, data security, and scalability, that healthcare organizations need to evaluate before choosing the most suitable option for their needs.</p></p>
