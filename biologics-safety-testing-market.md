<p><p>Biologics Safety Testing Market involves various types of tests to ensure the safety and quality of biologics products. Endotoxin tests are conducted to detect the presence of harmful bacterial toxins. Sterility tests check for the absence of viable microorganisms. Cell line authentication and characterization tests verify the identity and purity of cell lines used in production. Bioburden tests quantify the microbial load in samples. Residual host contaminant detection tests identify impurities from the host organism. Adventitious agent detection tests detect any potential contaminants. Other tests may include stability testing, virus screening, and mycoplasma testing.</p></p>
