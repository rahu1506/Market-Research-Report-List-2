<p><p>Noise cancelling earmuffs are a type of hearing protection device designed to reduce external noise by actively cancelling out or muffling the sound waves. These earmuffs are equipped with microphones that detect external noise and then generate an out-of-phase sound wave to cancel it out. They are primarily used in noisy environments such as construction sites, factories, airports, and other industrial settings.</p><p>The global market for noise cancelling earmuffs has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. This can be attributed to the increasing awareness about hearing protection and the rising concern for workplace safety. <p><p>The noise cancelling earmuffs market can be divided into two main types: headband earmuffs and wraparound earmuffs. Headband earmuffs consist of a band that goes over the head, with ear cups on each end to cover the ears. They provide a comfortable fit and are usually adjustable. On the other hand, wraparound earmuffs are designed to wrap around the back of the head and often have a neckband for added stability. They offer a secure fit and are preferred in situations where head movement is expected, such as during physical activities or in windy conditions.</p></p>
