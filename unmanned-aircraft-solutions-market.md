Rotary wing aircraft, also known as rotorcraft, use spinning rotors to generate lift and are capable of vertical takeoff and landing. The "others" category includes various types of unmanned aircraft that do not fit into the fixed wing or rotary wing classifications. These different types cater to various applications and offer diverse capabilities in the unmanned aircraft solutions market.</p></p>
