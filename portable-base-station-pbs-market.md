<p><p>A Portable Base Station (PBS) is a mobile communication system used for establishing communication networks in remote or emergency areas. The VHF (Very High Frequency) market refers to PBS that operates in the frequency range of 30-300 MHz, providing long-range communication with better penetration through obstacles. The UHF (Ultra High Frequency) market, on the other hand, covers the frequency range of 300-3000 MHz, offering shorter-range communication but with better signal quality and higher data transmission rates. Both VHF and UHF PBS serve different communication needs based on frequency characteristics and coverage requirements.</p></p>
