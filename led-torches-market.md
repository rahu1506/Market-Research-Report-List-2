<p><p>LED torches are available in two main market types: rechargeable LED torches and dry cell LED torches. </p><p>Rechargeable LED torches are equipped with a built-in rechargeable battery that can be easily charged using a power source. These torches offer the convenience of not having to constantly replace batteries and are more environmentally friendly.</p><p>On the other hand, dry cell LED torches rely on disposable batteries for power. These torches are suitable for situations where access to electricity for recharging is limited or unavailable.</p><p>Overall, consumers can choose between rechargeable LED torches for convenience and sustainability or opt for dry cell LED torches for situations that require battery-powered lighting.</p></p>
