<p><p>Smart home security refers to a system that combines home automation and security features to provide homeowners with enhanced control and monitoring of their homes. <p><p>The Smart Home Security market includes three main types: Video Surveillance System, Alarm System, and Access Control System. Video Surveillance System involves the use of cameras to monitor and record activities inside and outside the house. Alarm System detects any unauthorized entry, fire, or gas leak, and immediately alerts the homeowner or security service provider. Access Control System restricts or grants access to specific areas of the house through methods like key cards or biometric identification. These types of systems aim to enhance residential security and protect against potential threats.</p></p>
