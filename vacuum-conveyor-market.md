<p><strong>The Vacuum Conveyor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Type</li><li>Split Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum conveyor market types include integral type and split type. Integral type vacuum conveyors are designed as an all-in-one system, with the vacuum pump and conveying unit being integrated into a single unit. This type is typically more compact and convenient for small-scale applications. On the other hand, split type vacuum conveyors have the vacuum pump and conveying unit as separate components, offering flexibility in installation and the ability to connect multiple conveying units to a single vacuum pump, making it suitable for larger-scale operations.</p></p>
