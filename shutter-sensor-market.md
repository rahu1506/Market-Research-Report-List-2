<p><p>The shutter sensor market offers two main types: wired and wireless shutter sensors. Wired shutter sensors are connected to a central control panel through wires, allowing for a reliable and uninterrupted connection. On the other hand, wireless shutter sensors operate without physical connections, using radio frequency or Wi-Fi signals to communicate with the control panel. Wireless sensors provide flexibility in installation and can easily be repositioned as needed. Both types serve the purpose of detecting unauthorized opening or closing of shutters to enhance security systems.</p></p>
