<p><strong>The Computer Cases Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Case</li><li>Horizontal Case</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Computer cases can be categorized based on their orientation as vertical cases and horizontal cases. Vertical cases are designed to stand upright, taking up less space and providing better airflow. They are popular among gamers and users who want a sleek and compact setup. On the other hand, horizontal cases lay flat on the surface, making them suitable for small spaces like media centers. They often have additional features like built-in storage or space for multiple devices. The choice between vertical and horizontal cases depends on the user's preferences, available space, and intended use of the computer.</p></p>
