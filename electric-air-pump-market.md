<p><p>The Electric Air Pump Market is divided into two types: AC Electric Pump and DC Electric Pump. AC Electric Pump refers to the pumps that operate on alternating current, typically used for larger inflatables such as air mattresses, swimming pools, and inflatable boats. On the other hand, DC Electric Pump runs on direct current and is commonly used for smaller inflatables like air beds, balloons, and inflatable toys. Both types serve the purpose of quickly inflating or deflating objects by utilizing electric power.</p></p>
