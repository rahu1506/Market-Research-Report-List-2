<p><p>Repeater pipettes are commonly used in laboratories to dispense small volumes of liquid accurately and repetitively. There are two main types of repeater pipettes in the market: electronic repeater pipettes and manual repeater pipettes. Electronic repeater pipettes use motorized mechanisms and digital displays to help control the liquid dispensing process, ensuring higher precision and reducing user fatigue. On the other hand, manual repeater pipettes rely on manual operation, generally with adjustable settings, and are a more cost-effective option. Both types offer convenience and efficiency in liquid handling tasks.</p></p>
