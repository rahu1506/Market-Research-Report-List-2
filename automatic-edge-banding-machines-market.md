<p><p>Automatic edge banding machines are used in the woodworking industry to apply edges to furniture panels. There are two types of automatic edge banding machines: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they can perform all the necessary functions, including gluing, trimming, and finishing. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for tasks like feeding panels and changing tools. Both types of machines offer efficient and precise edge banding solutions, but fully automatic machines are generally more expensive and suitable for high production volumes.</p></p>
