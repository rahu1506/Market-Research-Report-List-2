<p><p>The Marine Oily Water Separators market consists of four major types: Gravity Oil Water Separator, Electrochemical Oil Water Separator, Bioremediation Oil Water Separator, and Centrifugal Oil Water Separator. Gravity separators utilize gravity to separate oil from water through the principle of density difference. Electrochemical separators use electrodes to attract and separate oil from water. Bioremediation separators use microorganisms to break down and remove oil contaminants. Centrifugal separators use centrifugal force to separate oil and water by spinning them at high speeds.</p></p>
