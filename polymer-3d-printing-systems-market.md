<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Polymer 3D printing systems are categorized into three types. Resin-based 3D printing systems use liquid resins that are hardened by UV light or lasers to create objects. Powder-based 3D printing systems use powdered polymers that are selectively fused together using lasers or binders to form objects. Filament 3D printing systems extrude molten polymers through nozzles to build objects layer by layer. Each of these systems offers unique advantages and can be used for various applications in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
