<p><p>The automotive ventilated seat market is divided into two types: suction type and air supply type. The suction type ventilated seats use suction fans to draw air from the seat and create a cooling effect. On the other hand, the air supply type seats introduce cool air through perforations in the seat surface using a dedicated air conditioning system. Both types aim to provide comfort and regulate temperature during hot weather, enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
