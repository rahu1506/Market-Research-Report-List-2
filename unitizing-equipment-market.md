<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic Unit Load Device</li><li>Automatic Unit Load Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unitizing equipment market consists of two main types: Semi-Automatic Unit Load Device (ULD) and Automatic ULD. Semi-Automatic ULDs require some manual intervention along with the use of equipment to create unitized loads for transportation or storage purposes. On the other hand, Automatic ULDs are fully automated systems that can efficiently and swiftly create unitized loads without requiring much human intervention. Both types serve the purpose of streamlining logistics operations and ensuring the safe transportation of goods.</p></p>
