<p><p>The industrial gaskets market consists of three main types: metallic, non-metallic, and semi-metallic gaskets. Metallic gaskets are made from materials such as stainless steel or copper and are suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Non-metallic gaskets are composed of materials like rubber or graphite and are commonly used in low-pressure settings. Semi-metallic gaskets combine the characteristics of both metallic and non-metallic gaskets, typically featuring a metal core with non-metallic covering layers. These types cater to different industrial requirements based on pressure, temperature, and chemical compatibility.</p></p>
