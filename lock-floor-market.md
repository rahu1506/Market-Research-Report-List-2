<p><ul><li>Side-Mount Floor Locks</li><li>Top-Mount Floor Locks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lock floor market types include side-mount floor locks and top-mount floor locks. Side-mount floor locks are designed to be attached to the side of the equipment or machinery and provide stability by locking the wheels in place. On the other hand, top-mount floor locks are installed on the top surface of the equipment, securing it firmly to the floor. Both types play a crucial role in preventing the unwanted movement of heavy machinery, ensuring safety and stability in industrial settings.</p></p>
