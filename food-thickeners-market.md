<p><strong>The Food Thickeners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polysaccharides-based Thickener</li><li>Protein-based Thickener</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food thickeners are substances added to food products to enhance their viscosity and texture. Polysaccharide-based thickeners are derived from carbohydrates like starches and gums, and they work by absorbing water and swelling, resulting in a thicker consistency. Protein-based thickeners, on the other hand, are derived from animal or plant proteins and create a gel-like texture when mixed with liquid. Both types of thickeners are commonly used in various food applications to improve mouthfeel, stability, and overall appeal of the product.</p></p>
