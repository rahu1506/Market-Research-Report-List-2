<p><p>High purity copper foil refers to copper foil that has a high degree of purity, typically above 99.97%. It is used in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive, among others. There are two main types of high purity copper foil: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through a rolling process, resulting in a thin and flexible foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced by an electroplating process, which ensures a high level of purity and uniform thickness. Both types are valued for their excellent electrical conductivity and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
