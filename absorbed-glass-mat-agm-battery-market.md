<p><p>An Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) battery is a type of rechargeable battery that consists of a glass mat separator between the battery plates. This separator is saturated with an electrolyte solution, which enables the free movement of ions between the plates. AGM batteries are designed to be maintenance-free and provide high performance in terms of power delivery and cycling capabilities.</p><p>The AGM battery market has been experiencing significant growth due to its increasing adoption in various applications, including automotive, aerospace, telecommunications, and renewable energy systems. The market growth can be attributed to the advantages offered by AGM batteries, such as longer life span, quick recharge, high energy density, and ability to withstand harsh environments.</p><p>One of the major trends observed in the AGM battery market is the rising demand for automotive batteries. The automotive sector is witnessing a shift towards AGM batteries due to their ability to provide high cranking power and deep cycling capabilities, making them suitable for start-stop systems and hybrid vehicles.
