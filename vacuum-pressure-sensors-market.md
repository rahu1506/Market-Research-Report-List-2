<p><p>Vacuum pressure sensors are devices used to measure and monitor pressure levels in a vacuum environment. There are various types available in the market. Piezo sensors use the piezoelectric effect to detect pressure changes and convert them into electrical signals. Pirani sensors rely on the thermal conductivity of gases to determine pressure levels. CDG sensors, also known as capacitance diaphragm gauges, utilize the change in capacitance between two diaphragms as pressure changes. Each type of sensor offers unique features and applications for accurate and efficient vacuum pressure measurement.</p></p>
