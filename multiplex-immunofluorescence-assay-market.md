<p><p>Multiplex immunofluorescence assay is a diagnostic technique used to detect and quantify multiple target molecules simultaneously in a single tissue sample. It involves the use of fluorescently-labeled antibodies to bind specifically to the target molecules, which can then be visualized under a microscope. This technique provides valuable information about the spatial distribution and co-localization of different biomarkers within a tissue, enabling researchers and clinicians to gain deeper insights into various diseases, including cancer.</p><p>The multiplex immunofluorescence assay market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
