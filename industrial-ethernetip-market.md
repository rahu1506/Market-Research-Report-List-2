<p><p>Industrial Ethernet/IP is a communication protocol that is specifically designed for industrial automation applications. It is an extension of traditional Ethernet, enabling real-time communication between industrial devices such as programmable logic controllers (PLCs), input/output (I/O) devices, and other industrial control systems. This protocol allows for seamless integration of industrial devices with enterprise-level networks, making it a fundamental component of the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT).</p><p>The Industrial Ethernet/IP market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing adoption of IIoT and the need for seamless communication among various industrial devices are the primary factors driving market growth.
