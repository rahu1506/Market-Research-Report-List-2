<p><p>The mountain bike suspension spring market offers two types of springs: air spring and coil spring. An air spring uses compressed air to provide suspension. It is lightweight and adjustable, allowing riders to customize the spring rate and sag. On the other hand, a coil spring uses a metal coil to absorb impacts and provide suspension. It is durable and preferred by riders who seek a more traditional and consistent suspension feel. Both market types cater to different preferences and riding styles, offering a variety of options for mountain bike enthusiasts.</p></p>
