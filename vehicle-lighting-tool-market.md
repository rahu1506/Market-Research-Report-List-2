<p><ul><li>Halogen Lighting Tool</li><li>HID Lighting Tool</li><li>LED Lighting Tool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle lighting tool market comprises three main types: Halogen lighting tools, HID lighting tools, and LED lighting tools. Halogen lighting tools use a tungsten filament to produce light and are the most common type. HID lighting tools use a combination of gases and electrical discharge to create light and are known for their high intensity. LED lighting tools use light-emitting diodes to produce light and are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and offer a wide range of color options. These three types of lighting tools cater to different preferences and needs in the vehicle lighting industry.</p></p>
