<p><p>Patient-controlled analgesic infusion pumps are used to deliver pain medication to patients in a controlled manner. There are two main types of these pumps - syringe-type and cassette-based fluid delivery type. Syringe-type pumps use a pre-filled syringe to deliver medication, while cassette-based pumps use a disposable cassette that contains the medication. Both types allow patients to self-administer pain medication by pressing a button when they require relief. These pumps provide a convenient and effective way to manage pain while minimizing the risk of medication errors and improving patient satisfaction.</p></p>
