<p><ul><li>Strabismic Lazy Eye</li><li>Refractive Lazy Eye</li><li>Deprivation Lazy Eye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lazy Eye Treatment market consists of three main types: Strabismic Lazy Eye, Refractive Lazy Eye, and Deprivation Lazy Eye. Strabismic Lazy Eye occurs due to misaligned eyes, causing one eye to become weaker. Refractive Lazy Eye is caused by refractive errors, such as nearsightedness or farsightedness, leading to blurred vision in one eye. Deprivation Lazy Eye happens when an infant's vision is blocked or obstructed, causing the brain to favor the other eye. These different types require specific treatments and interventions to improve vision and alignment.</p></p>
