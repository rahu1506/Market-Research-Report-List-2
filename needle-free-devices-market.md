<p><ul><li>Spring-Loaded Jet Injector</li><li>Battery-Powered Jet Injector</li><li>Gas-Powered Jet Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle-free device market is categorized into three types: spring-loaded jet injectors, battery-powered jet injectors, and gas-powered jet injectors. Spring-loaded jet injectors use a spring mechanism to create pressure and force liquid medication into the skin. Battery-powered jet injectors utilize a battery to power the injection process, propelling the medication into the skin. Gas-powered jet injectors use compressed gas to generate high-pressure streams of medication for injection, eliminating the need for needles. These devices offer painless and convenient drug delivery alternatives to traditional needle injections.</p></p>
