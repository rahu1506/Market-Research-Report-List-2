<p><strong>The Laser Microchannel Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stackable Type</li><li>Array Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser microchannel coolers are devices used to dissipate heat generated by laser systems. The stackable type coolers consist of individual cooling units that can be stacked together to increase cooling capacity. This type is suitable for applications requiring high power and multiple lasers. On the other hand, array type coolers consist of a single cooling unit with multiple microchannels. This design allows for effective cooling of a single laser or a small array of lasers. The choice between these types depends on the cooling requirements of the specific laser system.</p></p>
