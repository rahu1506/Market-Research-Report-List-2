<p><p>Self-regulating heating cables are used for maintaining a consistent temperature in various industries. There are three types of self-regulating heating cables, namely CABT, CAMT, and CAHT. CABT cables are designed for applications that require a low-temperature range, such as frost protection. CAMT cables are suitable for medium-temperature applications, including pipe or vessel freeze protection. CAHT cables, on the other hand, are high-temperature cables used for maintaining constant temperature in extreme conditions, such as process temperature maintenance in industrial environments. These cables provide efficient and reliable heating solutions for different temperature ranges.</p></p>
