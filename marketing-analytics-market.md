<p><p>Marketing analytics is the practice of measuring, managing, and analyzing data from marketing campaigns to improve marketing effectiveness and ROI. This approach helps marketers understand the impact of different channels on customer engagement and conversion rates, enabling them to allocate resources more efficiently.</p><p>In conclusion, the marketing analytics market is expected to experience significant growth, driven by the adoption of digital advertising, real-time analytics, and the need for personalized marketing.
