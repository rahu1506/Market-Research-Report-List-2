<p><p>The electric bidet seats market can be classified into three types: tank, tankless, and hybrid. Tank bidet seats are equipped with a water reservoir that heats and stores water for subsequent use. Tankless bidet seats, on the other hand, have an instant heating system that heats water on demand, eliminating the need for a water reservoir. Hybrid bidet seats combine the features of both tank and tankless models, offering the convenience of instant heating while also providing a supplementary water tank for extended use.</p></p>
