<p><strong>The Syringe Infusion Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Syringe infusion pumps are medical devices used to deliver fluids or medication into a patient's body in a controlled manner. There are two types of syringe infusion pumps: single-channel and dual-channel. </p><p>Single-channel pumps have one syringe chamber and are used when only one fluid or medication needs to be administered. Dual-channel pumps, on the other hand, have two separate syringe chambers, allowing the simultaneous administration of two different fluids or medications. This feature is particularly useful in situations where multiple drugs or fluids need to be delivered simultaneously, such as in critical care units or during surgery.</p></p>
