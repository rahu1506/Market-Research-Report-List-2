<p><p>Differentially pumped rotary feedthroughs are devices used in vacuum systems to transfer rotational motion through a vacuum-sealed environment. There are two main types: step motor differentially pumped rotary feedthroughs and manual differentially pumped rotary feedthroughs. Step motor feedthroughs utilize a stepper motor to control the rotation, offering precise and automated movements. On the other hand, manual feedthroughs require human intervention to rotate the device, providing more flexibility and control. Both types find applications in various industries that require vacuum-sealed rotational motion under different operational requirements.</p></p>
