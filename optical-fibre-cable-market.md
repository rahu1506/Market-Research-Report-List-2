<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The optical fibre cable market consists of two main types: single mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single mode fiber is designed for long-distance communication, transmitting a single light signal through a small core. It offers higher bandwidth and is mostly used in telecommunications and data centers. On the other hand, multimode fiber is designed for short-range communications, transmitting multiple light signals through a larger core. It provides lower bandwidth but is more cost-effective and commonly used for local area networks and short-distance connections.</p></p>
