<p><p>The cold chain market consists of two main types: refrigerated storage and cold chain logistics. Refrigerated storage involves the storage and preservation of products in cold storage facilities, such as warehouses, to maintain their freshness and quality. On the other hand, cold chain logistics focuses on the transportation and distribution of temperature-sensitive products, ensuring their proper handling and delivery throughout the supply chain. Both these types are crucial for industries like food and pharmaceuticals to maintain product integrity and safety.</p></p>
