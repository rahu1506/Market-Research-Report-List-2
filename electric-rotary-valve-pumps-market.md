This design allows for efficient and reliable pumping of various substances, including liquids, gases, and even solids.</p><p>The market for electric rotary valve pumps has been witnessing steady growth due to several factors. One of the key drivers is the increasing demand from industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, oil and gas, and water and wastewater treatment.
