<p><p>The cryotherapy market comprises different types such as cryosurgical, cryotherapy chamber, and others. Cryosurgical refers to the use of extreme cold temperatures to freeze and destroy abnormal tissues, commonly used in dermatology and oncology. Cryotherapy chambers involve exposing the whole body or specific body parts to extremely low temperatures for therapeutic purposes, mainly used for muscle recovery and pain management. The "others" category includes various forms of cryotherapy, like localized cryotherapy and cryofacial, which aim to provide localized cooling effects for specific conditions or aesthetic purposes.</p></p>
