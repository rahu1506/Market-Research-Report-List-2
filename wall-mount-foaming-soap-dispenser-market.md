<p><p>The wall mount foaming soap dispenser market can be categorized into two types: contact and non-contact. In a contact dispensing system, users have to physically touch the dispenser to activate the foam release. This type is commonly found in public restrooms and requires direct contact for operation. On the other hand, non-contact dispensers use sensors to detect hand movements and release the foam automatically, eliminating the need for physical contact. These touchless dispensers are considered more hygienic and are commonly found in hospitals, restaurants, and other high-traffic areas.</p></p>
