<p><p>The Intelligent Marking System market includes two main types: laser marking and inkjet marking. Laser marking utilizes a powerful laser beam to create permanent markings on materials like metals, plastics, and ceramics. It offers high precision and durability, making it suitable for industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics. On the other hand, inkjet marking employs an inkjet printer to spray tiny droplets of ink onto the desired surface, allowing for easy customization and variable data printing. Inkjet marking is commonly used in industries like packaging, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
