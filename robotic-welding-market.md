<p><p>Robotic welding refers to the use of automated robots to perform welding operations. The market for robotic welding is divided into different types based on the welding techniques used. Arc welding utilizes an electric arc between an electrode and the workpiece to melt and join metal components. Spot welding involves creating a series of quick and localized electric welds between two sheets of metal. Other types include resistance welding, laser welding, and friction welding, which all have their unique applications and benefits in the welding industry.</p></p>
