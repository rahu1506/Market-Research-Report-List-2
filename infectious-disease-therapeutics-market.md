<p><p>Infectious Disease Therapeutics market consists of two primary sectors: drugs and vaccines. Drugs are medications used to treat infectious diseases by targeting and killing the pathogens causing the illness. They can include antibiotics, antiviral drugs, antifungal medications, etc. On the other hand, vaccines are preventive measures that stimulate the body's immune response to specific pathogens, thus providing immunity against the disease. Vaccines are generally recommended for individuals to prevent infectious diseases and often involve the administration of weakened or killed pathogens or their parts.</p></p>
