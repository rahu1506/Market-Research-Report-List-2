<p><p>The rafting boat market comprises three main types: inflatable keel boat, rigid inflatable boat (RIB), and specialty boat. Inflatable keel boats are lightweight and easy to transport, featuring an inflatable chamber underneath the boat, providing stability in rough waters. RIBs have a sturdy hull made of fiberglass or aluminum and inflatable tubes around the sides, offering excellent maneuverability and durability. Specialty boats are designed for specific purposes like whitewater rafting or fishing, built with specialized features to meet the demands of these activities. These different types cater to various needs and preferences within the rafting boat market.</p></p>
