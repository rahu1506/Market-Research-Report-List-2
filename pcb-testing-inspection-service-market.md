<p><p>PCB Testing & Inspection Service Market involves various types of tests to ensure the quality and functionality of printed circuit boards (PCBs). X-Ray test utilizes X-ray radiation to inspect solder joints, component placement, and internal structures. The Flying Probe Test utilizes moving probes to test the connectivity of PCBs without requiring a test fixture. Functional Test verifies the PCB's behavior and performance under normal operating conditions. In-circuit (ICT) Test checks the electrical characteristics and behavior of individual components. Other tests include boundary scan and automated optical inspection, which detect faults and defects in PCBs.</p></p>
