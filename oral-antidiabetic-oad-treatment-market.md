<p><p>The Oral Antidiabetic (OAD) Treatment market consists of various types of medications aimed at managing blood sugar levels in individuals with diabetes. One category is Sulphonylureas which stimulate insulin production in the pancreas. Biguanides, on the other hand, reduce glucose production in the liver and improve insulin sensitivity. Intestinal α-Glucosidase Inhibitors slow down carbohydrate absorption in the intestines. The "Others" category includes various OAD medications with different mechanisms of action, such as meglitinides, thiazolidinediones, and dipeptidyl peptidase-4 inhibitors.
