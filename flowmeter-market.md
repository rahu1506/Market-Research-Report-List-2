<p><strong>The Flowmeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Ultrasonic Type</li><li>Magnetic Type</li><li>Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flowmeter market is comprised of various types including thermal, mechanical, ultrasonic, magnetic, and pressure types. Thermal flowmeters measure flow rate based on the amount of heat dissipated by a fluid. Mechanical flowmeters use mechanical components to measure flow rate, like spinning vanes or rotating gears. Ultrasonic flowmeters employ ultrasonic waves to measure flow rate through the Doppler or transit-time principle. Magnetic flowmeters measure flow rate using Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction. Pressure flowmeters determine flow rate by the pressure difference across an obstruction in the flow path. These different types cater to a wide range of applications and industries.</p></p>
