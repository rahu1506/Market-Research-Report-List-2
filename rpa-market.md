<p><p>RPA, or Robotic Process Automation, is an emerging technology that uses software robots or artificial intelligence to automate business processes. Several factors contribute to this growth. Firstly, the increasing need for cost reduction and operational efficiency drives organizations to adopt RPA solutions. RPA platforms provide data analytics capabilities that help businesses gain valuable insights, identify trends, and make informed decisions. Additionally, RPA enables process optimization by streamlining workflows, reducing errors, and enhancing overall productivity.</p><p>Furthermore, the adoption of cloud-based RPA solutions is on the rise as organizations look for flexible and scalable automation solutions. Cloud-based RPA allows businesses to deploy and manage bots centrally, making it easier to scale and monitor automation processes.</p><p>Moreover, the integration of RPA with technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) is a key trend in the market. AI and ML algorithms enable RPA bots to learn from human actions, making them more intelligent and capable of handling complex tasks.</p><p>Overall, the RPA market is witnessing steady growth due to the need for cost reduction, process optimization, and the integration of advanced technologies. The projected CAGR of 7.2% reflects the market's potential for expansion in the coming years.</p></p>
