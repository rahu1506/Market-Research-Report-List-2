<p><p>The hearing instruments market offers different types of devices to assist people with hearing loss. Behind-The-Ear (BTE) devices are worn behind the ear and connected to a custom earmold within the ear canal. In-The-Ear (ITE) devices fit entirely within the outer ear. In-The-Canal (ITC) devices are smaller and sit partially in the ear canal. Completely-In-Canal (CIC) devices are the smallest and fit entirely inside the ear canal. Each market segment caters to different hearing needs and preferences, providing a range of options for individuals seeking hearing aid solutions.</p></p>
