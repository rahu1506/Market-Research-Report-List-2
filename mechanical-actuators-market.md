<p><p>Mechanical actuators are devices that convert electrical, hydraulic, or pneumatic energy into mechanical motion to perform various tasks. Linear actuators produce straight-line motion, commonly used in robotics, automotive applications, and industrial machinery. Rotary actuators produce rotational motion, useful in valves, pumps, and robotics. Other types of actuators include multi-axis actuators, which offer motion in multiple directions, and micro actuators, which are small-scale devices used in medical equipment or microelectronics. These actuators enable precise and controlled movement in a wide range of industries for improved performance and automation.</p></p>
