<p><p>In the power lightning protection box market, there are two main types: tandem type and parallel type. Tandem type protection boxes consist of multiple surge protectors connected in series, offering sequential protection to electrical equipment. On the other hand, parallel type protection boxes have surge protectors connected in parallel, providing simultaneous and independent protection for each circuit. Both types aim to safeguard electrical devices from power surges caused by lightning strikes, but they differ in their protective arrangements and the level of coordination among the surge protectors.</p></p>
