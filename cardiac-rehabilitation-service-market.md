<p><p>The Cardiac Rehabilitation Service market includes two main types: Outpatient Rehabilitation Programs and Inpatient Rehabilitation Programs. Outpatient Rehabilitation Programs are designed for patients who have been discharged from the hospital after a cardiac event but still require ongoing care. These programs typically involve regular visits to a rehabilitation center where patients undergo exercise training, education, and counseling to improve their heart health. In contrast, Inpatient Rehabilitation Programs are provided to patients who require more intensive care and monitoring, typically in a hospital setting, due to the severity of their cardiac condition.</p></p>
