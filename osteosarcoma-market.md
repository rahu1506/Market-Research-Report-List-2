<p><p>Osteosarcoma is a type of bone cancer that can be categorized into three market types: intramedullary osteosarcoma, juxtacortical osteosarcoma, and extraskeletal osteosarcoma. Intramedullary osteosarcoma refers to tumors that develop within the bone marrow, while juxtacortical osteosarcoma refers to tumors that occur on the surface of the bone. Lastly, extraskeletal osteosarcoma refers to tumors that originate outside the bones, typically in soft tissues. These different market types help classify and understand the various presentations of osteosarcoma, aiding in diagnosis, treatment, and research.</p></p>
