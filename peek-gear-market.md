<p><p>The PEEK gear market comprises various types of gears that are used in different industries. Spur gears are ideal for applications that require high-speed and low noise levels. Helical gears, with their inclined teeth, provide smoother and quieter operation, making them suitable for heavy-load and high-speed applications. Bevel gears are used to transmit rotational force between non-parallel intersecting shafts. Other PEEK gears include planetary gears, rack and pinion gears, and worm gears, each designed for specific applications. The PEEK gear market offers a wide range of options to cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
