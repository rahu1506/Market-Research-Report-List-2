<p><p>The Turbo Molecular Pumps market can be classified into three major types: Magnetically Suspended Type, Oil Lubricated Type, and Others. Magnetically Suspended Type pumps use magnetic bearings to levitate the rotor, eliminating the need for mechanical contact and reducing friction. Oil Lubricated Type pumps rely on oil lubrication for smooth operation. The Other category includes alternative pump technologies that don't fit in the previous two categories. These types offer different advantages and are used in various industries for creating high vacuum environments and facilitating efficient gas flow.</p></p>
