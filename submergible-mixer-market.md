<p><p>The submergible mixer market comprises two main types: monophase and threephase. Monophase mixers are designed to operate using a single phase power supply, making them suitable for smaller applications and easy to install. On the other hand, threephase mixers require a three-phase power supply and are commonly used in large-scale industrial applications due to their higher power capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in mixing operations, ensuring efficient and uniform blending of substances underwater.</p></p>
