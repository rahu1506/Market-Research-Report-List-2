<p><strong>The Pool Purification System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Disinfection</li><li>Pump Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pool purification system market includes two main types: water disinfection and pump filtration. Water disinfection systems are designed to kill harmful bacteria and viruses in pool water, ensuring its cleanliness and safety for swimmers. These systems typically use chemicals like chlorine or UV light to eliminate contaminants. On the other hand, pump filtration systems are responsible for removing debris, dirt, and other particles from the water. They use filtration mechanisms such as sand, cartridges, or diatomaceous earth to trap and remove impurities, keeping the pool water clear and visually appealing.</p></p>
