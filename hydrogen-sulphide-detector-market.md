<p><p>The hydrogen sulphide detector market consists of two main types: fixed type and portable type detectors. Fixed type detectors are designed to be installed in a fixed location, such as industrial facilities or confined spaces, to continuously monitor the presence of hydrogen sulphide gas. These detectors provide real-time alerts in the event of a gas leak, ensuring the safety of the surrounding environment. On the other hand, portable type detectors are handheld devices that can be carried around by users for on-the-go applications. These detectors are commonly used by workers in industries like oil and gas, mining, and wastewater treatment to detect and measure hydrogen sulphide gas levels in their surrounding environments.</p></p>
