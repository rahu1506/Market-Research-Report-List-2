<p><p>Automotive Thermoplastic Resin Composites market includes various types such as Glass Mat Thermoplastic (GMT), Short Fiber Thermoplastic (SFT), Long Fiber Thermoplastic (LFT), Continuous Fiber Thermoplastic (CFT), and others. GMT consists of randomly oriented glass fibers in a thermoplastic matrix, SFT uses short fibers for reinforcement, LFT involves longer fibers for improved mechanical properties, and CFT features continuous fibers for high strength and stiffness. Each type offers unique benefits suited for different automotive applications.</p></p>
