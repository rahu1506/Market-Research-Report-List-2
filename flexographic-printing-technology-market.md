<p><p>Flexographic printing technology is used for printing on various materials such as plastic films, paper, and cardboard. The market can be classified into two types: semi-automatic and automatic. In the semi-automatic market, printing presses require manual intervention for tasks like plate loading, ink filling, and substrate feeding. On the other hand, the automatic market involves fully automated processes, where printing presses perform tasks without human intervention, resulting in higher productivity and efficiency. These two market types differ in the level of automation and the extent of human involvement in printing operations.</p></p>
