<p><p>Medical appointment scheduling software is available in two main market types: web-based and on-premise. </p><p>Web-based software is accessed through a web browser and does not require installation or maintenance on the user's device. It offers the advantage of remote accessibility, allowing healthcare providers to manage appointments from anywhere with an internet connection.</p><p>On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed and maintained on the user's own servers or computers. It offers greater control and customization options but requires technical expertise for installation and maintenance. It is typically preferred by larger healthcare organizations with dedicated IT departments.</p></p>
