<p><p>The tour guide system market consists of two main types: wire tour guide systems and wireless tour guide systems. Wire tour guide systems use physical cables to connect the tour guide's microphone and the participants' receivers, providing clear audio transmission. On the other hand, wireless tour guide systems utilize radio frequency technology to transmit audio signals without the need for cables, allowing participants to move freely during the tour. Both systems serve the purpose of enabling effective communication between a tour guide and their audience, enhancing the overall tour experience.</p></p>
