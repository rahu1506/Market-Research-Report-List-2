<p><p>The Wafer Level Chip Scale Package (WLCSP) market comprises two main types: Wafer Bumping and Shellcase. In the Wafer Bumping market, the package is created by attaching solder balls directly onto the wafer, simplifying the manufacturing process and reducing costs. On the other hand, the Shellcase market involves using a thin metal shell, which is then attached to the wafer, offering increased protection and durability. Both types provide compact and cost-effective packaging solutions for semiconductor devices.</p></p>
