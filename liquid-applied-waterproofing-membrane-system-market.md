<p><p>Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membrane System Market can be divided into two main types - NCM Type and NCA Type. NCM Type membranes are made up of two components that are mixed before application, offering a seamless and durable waterproofing solution. On the other hand, NCA Type membranes are single-component systems that are easier and quicker to apply, making them more suitable for projects with time constraints. Both types provide effective waterproofing protection for various surfaces and are widely used in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
