<p><p>Energy harvesting is a booming market that encompasses various types of technologies. Photovoltaic energy harvesting involves using solar panels to convert sunlight into electricity. Thermoelectric energy harvesting harnesses temperature differences to generate power. Piezo energy harvesting utilizes vibrations and mechanical pressure to produce electrical energy. Electrodynamic energy harvesting captures kinetic energy from motion and converts it into usable power. These technologies are gaining popularity as they provide sustainable and renewable energy solutions that can be integrated into various devices and systems.</p></p>
