<p><p>The pool cues market includes several types of cues. One-piece cues are solid and not detachable, offering a simple and traditional design. Two-piece cues consist of a shorter butt section and a longer shaft, joined in the middle, giving players the option to remove the butt for easier transportation. Middle two-piece cues have a joint closer to the center of the cue, providing more balance during play. Three-Quarter two-piece cues have the joint positioned closer to the butt, enabling players to remove a larger portion of the cue for enhanced portability.</p></p>
