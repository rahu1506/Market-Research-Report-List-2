<p><strong>The Print Quality Inspection & Verification System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line Type</li><li>Off-line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Print Quality Inspection & Verification System market can be categorized into two types: in-line type and off-line type. In-line type refers to systems that are integrated directly into the printing process, allowing for real-time inspection and verification of print quality as the printing is being done. This type of system provides immediate feedback and reduces the chances of defective prints. On the other hand, off-line type systems are separate systems that inspect and verify the print quality after the printing process is completed. These systems are useful for batch inspection, quality control, and post-production analysis. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific use case.</p></p>
