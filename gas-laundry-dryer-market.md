Gas laundry dryers are known for their efficiency and faster drying times compared to electric dryers.</p><p>The gas laundry dryer market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period. <p><p>The gas laundry dryer market consists of two main types: natural gas and liquid propane. Natural gas dryers are designed to be used with a natural gas supply, which is typically piped into homes and buildings. On the other hand, liquid propane dryers utilize propane as a fuel source, which is typically stored in tanks and needs to be refilled periodically. Both types of dryers provide efficient and effective drying capabilities, but the choice between the two depends on the availability and accessibility of natural gas or liquid propane in a particular area.</p></p>
