<p><strong>The qRT-PCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>qPCR</li><li>dPCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>qRT-PCR (quantitative reverse transcription polymerase chain reaction) is a technique used to measure gene expression levels by converting RNA into complementary DNA (cDNA) and then amplifying specific target sequences. The qPCR market refers to the market for the instruments and reagents used in this technique. On the other hand, dPCR (digital PCR) is a newer technology that partitions the PCR reaction into thousands of individual reactions, allowing for more precise quantification of target molecules. The dPCR market refers to the market for the instruments and reagents used in digital PCR techniques. Both qPCR and dPCR are widely used in research, diagnostics, and other applications.</p></p>
