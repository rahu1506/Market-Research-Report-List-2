<p><p>The low-voltage circuit breakers market is classified into three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are compact and commonly used in residential and commercial applications for protecting electrical circuits against overloads and short circuits. MCCBs are larger and provide higher ampere ratings, suitable for industrial and commercial settings. ACBs are designed for heavy-duty applications in industries, offering higher current ratings and advanced protection features. These circuit breakers play a vital role in safeguarding electrical systems and preventing damage from electrical faults.</p></p>
