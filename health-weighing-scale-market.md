<p><strong>The Health Weighing Scale Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Scale</li><li>Pointer Scale</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Health weighing scales come in various types, such as digital scales and pointer scales. Digital scales utilize electronic sensors to measure weight and provide a digital display of the reading. They offer higher accuracy and precision compared to pointer scales, which rely on a mechanical needle that moves to indicate weight. Pointer scales are typically less expensive but may have limited accuracy. Both types are commonly used in households and healthcare settings for monitoring body weight and managing health goals.</p></p>
