<p><p>Low pressure sealed pressure sensors are classified into different types based on their technology. The strain type sensors measure pressure by detecting the strain on a sensing element. Piezoresistive type sensors use a diaphragm with piezoresistive material, which changes its resistance under pressure. Capacitive type sensors measure pressure by detecting the change in capacitance between two plates as pressure is applied. Additionally, there are other types of low pressure sealed pressure sensors that use different technologies to measure pressure accurately.</p></p>
