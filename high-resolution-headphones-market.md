<p><p>The high resolution headphones market can be broadly classified into three types: in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear headphones. In-ear headphones, also known as earbuds, are compact and directly inserted into the ear canal. On-ear headphones rest on the ears and provide a balance between portability and sound quality. Over-ear headphones, also called full-sized headphones, cover the entire ear and offer superior sound isolation and immersive audio experience. Each type caters to different user preferences, with in-ear for convenience, on-ear for versatility, and over-ear for audiophiles seeking top-notch sound fidelity.</p></p>
