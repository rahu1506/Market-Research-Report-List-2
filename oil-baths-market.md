<p><strong>The Oil Baths Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating</li><li>Non-Circulating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil baths market consists of two main types: circulating and non-circulating. In circulating oil baths, a pump circulates the oil within the bath to ensure consistent temperature distribution. This type is commonly used in laboratories and industrial settings where precise and uniform heating is required. On the other hand, non-circulating oil baths do not have a pump, resulting in uneven temperature distribution. These baths are often used in applications where a consistent temperature is less critical, such as in general heating or warming processes.</p></p>
