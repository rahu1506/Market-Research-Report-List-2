<p><p>Renal Replacement Therapeutics (RRT) refers to the medical treatment methods used to replace the normal functioning of the kidneys in patients with kidney failure or end-stage renal disease (ESRD). This therapy is essential for removing waste products, toxins, and excess fluids from the body when the natural filtration process of the kidneys is compromised.</p><p>The global renal replacement therapeutics market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to various factors, including the increasing prevalence of chronic kidney diseases (CKD) and ESRD, rising geriatric population, and advancements in healthcare infrastructure and technology.</p><p>With the growing incidence of CKD and ESRD globally, the demand for renal replacement therapeutics is expected to rise. Furthermore, the rising preference for dialysis treatment over kidney transplantation due to limited availability of donor organs is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends that are shaping its landscape. One of the prominent trends is the increasing adoption of home-based peritoneal dialysis systems, as they offer convenience and cost-effectiveness compared to in-hospital hemodialysis. Moreover, technological advancements in this field, such as the development of wearable and portable dialysis devices, are further propelling market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the renal replacement therapeutics market is experiencing substantial growth, primarily due to the rising prevalence of kidney diseases and advancements in healthcare technology.
