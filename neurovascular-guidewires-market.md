<p><p>The Neurovascular Guidewire market consists of two main types - Solid Guidewire and Wrapped Guidewire. A Solid Guidewire is a flexible wire made of a single material, usually stainless steel or Nitinol, which provides stability and support during neurovascular procedures. On the other hand, a Wrapped Guidewire is covered with a polymer coating, which enhances the handling and maneuverability of the wire during procedures. Both types play a crucial role in navigating the complex blood vessels in the brain, enabling safe and effective treatment of neurovascular conditions.</p></p>
