<p><ul><li>MLV</li><li>MOV</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial varistors are electrical components used to protect electronic devices from voltage surges. There are three main types of radial varistors on the market: MLV (Multilayer Varistors), MOV (Metal Oxide Varistors), and Others. MLV varistors are made of multiple layers of ceramic material and offer high capacitance and energy absorption capabilities. MOV varistors are made of metal oxide and provide excellent protection against voltage spikes. The category "Others" includes varistors made of different materials or with specialized features, such as polymer-based, ceramic disk, or silicon-based varistors.</p></p>
