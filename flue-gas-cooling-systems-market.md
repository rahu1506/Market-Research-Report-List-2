<p><p>Flue gas cooling systems are designed to remove the excess heat from flue gas, which is generated during the combustion process in industries and power plants. These systems help to capture the heat and utilize it for various industrial processes while reducing thermal pollution. Flue gas cooling systems are commonly used in applications such as waste-to-energy plants, biomass plants, thermal power plants, and cement kilns.</p><p>The global flue gas cooling systems market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market is driven by factors such as increasing regulations on emissions and the growing demand for energy efficiency. Flue gas cooling systems help industries comply with emission standards by reducing the temperature and harmful pollutants present in flue gas, thereby mitigating environmental impact.</p><p>Furthermore, the adoption of flue gas cooling systems is growing due to the implementation of stringent emission norms by various governments across the globe.
