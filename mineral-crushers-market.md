<p><p>The mineral crushers market can be categorized into various types. Gyratory crushers are used for crushing materials by rotating a cone-shaped inner chamber, while jaw crushers have a fixed jaw and a movable one for crushing rocks. Cone crushers are similar to gyratory crushers but operate with a different geometry. Roll crushers consist of two counter-rotating rollers for crushing materials. Other types may include impact crushers or hammer crushers. Each of these crushers serve the purpose of reducing the size of minerals or rocks for further processing or utilization.</p></p>
