<p><p>The MBR (Membrane BioReactor) Membrane market encompasses three major types: Tubular Membrane, Hollow Fiber Membrane, and Flat Membrane. Tubular Membrane consists of small, cylindrical tubes that allow liquid to flow through while retaining solid particles. Hollow Fiber Membrane comprises bundles of small, hollow strands that filter impurities by allowing liquid to pass through their walls. Flat Membrane consists of a flat sheet with tiny pores that selectively separate solids from liquids. These membrane types are widely used in various industries for water treatment, wastewater management, and other filtration processes.</p></p>
