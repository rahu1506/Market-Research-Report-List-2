<p><p>The Apolipoprotein Test is a diagnostic blood test that measures the levels of apolipoproteins in the blood. Apolipoproteins are proteins that bind lipids, such as cholesterol and triglycerides, to form lipoproteins, which are responsible for transporting these lipids throughout the body. The test helps in assessing the risk of cardiovascular diseases, such as heart attacks and strokes, by evaluating the lipoprotein profile.</p><p>The Apolipoprotein Test Market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors.
