<p><p>The medical warming blanket market offers three main types of products: full body, lower body, and upper body blankets. Full body blankets provide complete coverage and warmth to the entire body, making them suitable for various medical procedures or post-surgical recovery. Lower body blankets specifically target the lower half of the body, including the legs, providing localized warming. Similarly, upper body blankets focus on the upper part of the body, such as the chest and shoulders, and are commonly used in surgeries or therapies requiring heating in these areas.</p></p>
