<p><strong>The No-clean Pastes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lead-containing</li><li>Lead Free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>No-clean pastes are used in electronic manufacturing to eliminate the need for post-soldering cleaning processes. There are two main types of no-clean pastes: lead-containing and lead-free. Lead-containing pastes contain a small amount of lead, which helps improve solderability and reduces the risk of tin whisker growth. On the other hand, lead-free pastes are environmentally friendly as they do not contain any lead, making them a preferred choice for companies looking to adhere to stricter environmental regulations. Both types of pastes offer similar benefits, but the decision to use one over the other depends on specific industry requirements and regulations.</p></p>
