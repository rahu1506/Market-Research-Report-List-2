<p><p>Wireless endoscopes are medical devices used for internal visual inspection of the body without the need for traditional cabled connections. Fixed focus endoscopes have a predetermined focal length and provide a clear image of a specific distance. They are suitable for examining a specific area. On the other hand, zoom focus endoscopes allow variable focusing to obtain clear images at different distances. They are versatile tools that offer flexibility in examining various body parts. Both types cater to different medical requirements and offer unique capabilities for diagnostic and surgical procedures.</p></p>
