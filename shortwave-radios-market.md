<p><p>Shortwave radios are widely used for long-distance communication and can be classified into different types. FM radios receive signals in the frequency modulation format, ensuring high-quality audio. FM/AM radios can receive signals in both frequency modulation and amplitude modulation formats, providing access to a wider range of radio stations. Other types of shortwave radios include single sideband (SSB) radios, which allow for greater transmission distances, and digital radios, which offer advanced features like digital signal processing. These different market types cater to various consumer preferences and needs for radio communication.</p></p>
