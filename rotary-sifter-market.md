<p><p>A rotary sifter, also known as a rotary screen or trommel screen, is a mechanical screening machine used to separate materials by size. Rotary sifters are widely used in various industries including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and mining.</p><p>The global rotary sifter market has been steadily growing in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period.
