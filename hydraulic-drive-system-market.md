<p><p>A hydraulic drive system is a type of power transmission system that uses fluid (usually oil) to transfer energy. The market types of hydraulic drive systems include hydraulic press, hydraulic cylinder, hydraulic motor, and hydraulic valves. </p><p>A hydraulic press is a machine that uses fluid power to generate compressive force for forming and shaping materials. </p><p>A hydraulic cylinder is a mechanical actuator used to provide linear force through a stroke in applications such as construction equipment and manufacturing machinery. </p><p>A hydraulic motor is a rotary actuator that converts hydraulic power into mechanical power to drive machinery or equipment. </p><p>Hydraulic valves are components that control the flow and pressure of hydraulic fluid in a hydraulic system, ensuring proper operation and safety.</p></p>
