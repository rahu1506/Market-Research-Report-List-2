<p><p>CHNS (O) Analyzer market types can be categorized into three main types: GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC Chromatography involves separating and analyzing the elements present in a sample using gas chromatography techniques. Frontal Chromatography is a process where a sample is passed through a column, and various components are separated based on their affinity to the stationary phase. Adsorption-Desorption utilizes adsorbent materials to capture and release the elements of interest, allowing for their analysis. These techniques are commonly used in laboratories and research settings for elemental analysis.</p></p>
