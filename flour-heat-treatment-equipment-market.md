<p><p>Flour heat treatment equipment is divided into two types: dry and wet. In the dry market, the equipment utilized is designed to remove moisture from the flour through the use of hot air. This process helps to enhance the shelf life of the flour and prevent the growth of bacteria. On the other hand, in the wet market, the equipment is used to treat the flour with water or steam. This process helps to modify the starch and protein structure in the flour, resulting in improved functionality and quality for various food applications.</p></p>
