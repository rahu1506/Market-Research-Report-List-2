<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor market consists of two main types: resonators and delay lines. Resonators are devices that generate an acoustic wave at a specific frequency when an electric signal is applied. They are used for applications such as frequency control in electronics and in telecommunications. Delay lines, on the other hand, are used to introduce a desired time delay in a signal. They find applications in communication systems, radar systems, and signal processing. Both resonators and delay lines play crucial roles in the SAW sensor market by enabling precise frequency control and time delay capabilities.</p></p>
