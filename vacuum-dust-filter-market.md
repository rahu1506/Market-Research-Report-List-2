<p><ul><li>Wet Filters</li><li>Dry Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vacuum Dust Filter Market comprises two main types: Wet Filters and Dry Filters. Wet Filters are designed to capture dust and debris using water as a filtration medium. They work by forcing the air through a water bath, trapping the particles in the liquid. On the other hand, Dry Filters use materials like foam, paper, or cloth to capture and retain dust. These filters work by allowing the air to pass through while collecting the particles in the filter material. Both types are essential for maintaining clean indoor air quality.</p></p>
