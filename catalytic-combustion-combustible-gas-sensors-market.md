<p><p>Catalytic combustion combustible gas sensors are used to detect and measure the presence of flammable gases in various applications. There are two types of markets for these sensors: diffusion and inhalation. </p><p>In diffusion market, the sensors are designed to be placed in the area where the gas is expected to accumulate. They rely on gas diffusion to reach the sensor surface for detection. </p><p>In inhalation market, the sensors are designed to be placed near the potential source of gas, such as industrial equipment or pipelines. They actively draw the gas into the sensor for detection. </p><p>Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safety by alerting to the presence of combustible gases and preventing potential hazards.</p></p>
