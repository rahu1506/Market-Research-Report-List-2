<p><p>The Portable RF Signal Generator market comprises two major types: Analog Signal Generator and Vector Signal Generator. Analog Signal Generators produce continuous waveforms and are used for basic testing purposes. They are affordable, simple to use, and widely applicable. On the other hand, Vector Signal Generators are more advanced and versatile, capable of generating complex signals for modern communication systems. They are essential for designing and testing wireless communication devices, offering greater accuracy, flexibility, and signal fidelity. Both types play crucial roles in the RF industry but cater to different needs and applications.</p></p>
