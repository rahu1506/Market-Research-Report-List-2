<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable operating tables are available in four main types: manual, electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic. Manual tables are operated by hand, with the user manually adjusting the height and position. Electric tables are powered by electricity, and can be adjusted with the push of a button. Hydraulic tables use a hydraulic system to adjust the height and angle, offering smooth and precise movements. Electro-hydraulic tables combine the benefits of electric and hydraulic systems, providing efficient and accurate adjustments through a combination of both technologies.</p></p>
