Firstly, there is a growing trend of DIY (do-it-yourself) activities and a resurgence of interest in sewing among hobbyists and enthusiasts. With the rise of social media platforms and the popularity of sewing blogs, people are increasingly engaging in sewing as a creative outlet. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has led to an increased demand for home sewing machines as people turned to sewing their own masks and other protective gear. This surge in demand is expected to continue even after the pandemic as people continue to prioritize personalization and uniqueness in their clothing.</p><p>Looking ahead, the future outlook for the home sewing machines market is promising. Technological advancements, such as the integration of smart features and connectivity options in sewing machines, are expected to drive market growth. Consumers are becoming more aware of the environmental impacts of fast fashion and are keen on creating their own sustainable garments. This shift in consumer behavior is anticipated to fuel the market growth as more individuals opt for home sewing machines to reduce their reliance on mass-produced clothing.</p><p>In conclusion, the home sewing machines market is positioned to grow at a CAGR of 4% during the forecasted period.
