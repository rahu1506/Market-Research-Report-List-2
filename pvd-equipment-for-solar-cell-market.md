<p><p>PVD equipment for solar cell market is mainly dominated by three types of technologies: Thermal Evaporation, Sputter Deposition, and Arc Vapor Deposition. Thermal Evaporation involves heating the source material until it evaporates and deposits onto the substrate. Sputter Deposition uses a high-energy plasma to eject atoms from a target material onto the substrate. Arc Vapor Deposition involves using an electric arc to vaporize and deposit the material onto the substrate. These technologies play a vital role in manufacturing high-quality solar cells.</p></p>
