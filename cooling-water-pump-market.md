<p><ul><li>Wet Cooling</li><li>Dry Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooling water pump market can be categorized into two types: wet cooling and dry cooling. Wet cooling systems use water as a cooling medium, circulating it through the condenser to remove heat from the system. Dry cooling systems, on the other hand, use air as the cooling medium, eliminating the need for water consumption. Both types serve the purpose of dissipating heat from industrial processes, with wet cooling being more common in industries that prioritize water conservation and dry cooling being preferred in areas with limited water availability or strict environmental regulations regarding water usage.</p></p>
