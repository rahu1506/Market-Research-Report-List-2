<p><p>The Lab Hydrogen Gas Generator market can be classified into two main types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer and PEM Electrolyzer. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers use alkaline solutions, typically potassium hydroxide, to split water into hydrogen and oxygen gases. They are known for their cost-effectiveness and long-term durability. On the other hand, PEM Electrolyzers employ a Proton Exchange Membrane to facilitate the separation of water molecules, resulting in high-purity hydrogen gas. These electrolyzers are more compact, efficient, and suitable for applications with high purity requirements.</p></p>
