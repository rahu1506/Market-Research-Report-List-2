<p><p>Thermal inkjet print-heads are commonly used in two main market types: continuous inkjet and random inkjet. Continuous inkjet systems involve a continuous stream of ink droplets being applied to the print surface, allowing for high-speed and precise printing. Random inkjet, on the other hand, involves the printing of individual droplets in a random pattern, making it ideal for more artistic or decorative applications. Both market types offer their own advantages and are widely used in different industries for various printing needs.</p></p>
