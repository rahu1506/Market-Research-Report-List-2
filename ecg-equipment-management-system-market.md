<p><p>The ECG Equipment and Management System market includes various types of devices. Holter Monitors are portable devices that record the patient's heart activity for extended periods. Resting ECG Systems are used to obtain a snapshot of the patient's heart activity during rest. Stress ECG Monitors are utilized during exercise or stress tests to evaluate the cardiovascular system's response to activity. Event Monitoring Systems are designed to detect and record abnormal heart rhythms as they occur. ECG Management Systems aid in the storage, analysis, and interpretation of ECG data. Other devices in the market include telemetry systems, wireless ECG systems, and remote monitoring solutions.</p></p>
