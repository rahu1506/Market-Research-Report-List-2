<p><p>The Urolithiasis Management Device Market comprises various types of devices used to treat kidney stones. Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL) involves using high-energy shock waves to break down kidney stones from outside the body. Intracorporeal Lithotripsy involves inserting a flexible device into the urinary tract to remove or break down stones. Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy is a surgical procedure where a small incision is made in the back to remove or break down large stones. The "Others" category includes alternative treatment methods or devices that are less commonly used for urolithiasis management.</p></p>
