<p><p>The dental gypsum market consists of three main types: dental plaster, model dental stone, and die dental stone. Dental plaster is a calcium sulfate hemihydrate powder used to create impressions of oral tissues for diagnostic purposes. Model dental stone, also known as dental stone plaster, is a stronger material used to create dental models for treatment planning and orthodontic purposes. Die dental stone, or die stone plaster, is the strongest type used to make precision replicas of teeth and dental structures for prosthetics and restorations. These three types serve different purposes in dental practice.</p></p>
