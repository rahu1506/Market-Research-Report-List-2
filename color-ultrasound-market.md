<p><p>The Color Ultrasound Market comprises two main types of systems: Hand-Carried Color Doppler Ultrasound Systems and Cart based Color Doppler Ultrasound Systems. Hand-Carried systems are small and portable, allowing for increased flexibility and point-of-care usage. They are commonly used in emergency medicine, radiology, and critical care.
