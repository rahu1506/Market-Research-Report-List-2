<p><p>Folding canes are a type of walking cane that can be folded into a compact size for easy transportation and storage. Firstly, the aging population has led to an increased demand for mobility aids, including folding canes. As the elderly population continues to grow, the market for folding canes is expected to expand further.</p><p>Additionally, the rising prevalence of mobility impairments and disabilities across all age groups is driving the demand for folding canes. A growing number of people with conditions such as arthritis, balance disorders, and injuries are opting for folding canes to improve their mobility and independence.</p><p>The market is also benefiting from technological advancements, with manufacturers introducing innovative features and designs to enhance the functionality and user experience of folding canes.
