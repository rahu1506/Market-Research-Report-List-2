<p><p>The yeast market consists of two main types: fresh yeast and dry yeast. Fresh yeast is a perishable product commonly used in bakeries and requires refrigeration for storage. It is available in different forms such as instant, active, and rapid rise yeast, and is widely used by home bakers and food manufacturers. Both fresh and dry yeast play a crucial role in the baking industry, providing leavening and enhancing the flavor of various dough-based products.</p></p>
