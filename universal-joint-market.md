<p><p>A universal joint is a mechanical device used to transmit power between two shafts that are not in line with each other. There are different types of universal joints available in the market. One type is the Constant Velocity Joint (CV joint), which allows smooth power transmission while maintaining a constant velocity. Another type is the Cross-axis Universal Joint, which is designed to transmit power at an angle between the input and output shafts. Both types of universal joints are commonly used in various industries for different applications where flexibility and efficient power transmission are required.</p></p>
