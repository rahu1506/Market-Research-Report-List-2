<p><p>The Medical & Hygiene PP Non Woven Fabric market consists of different types: Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid. Drylaid refers to a process where fibers are laid down using air or mechanical means without any wet processes. Airlaid involves using air to form a web of fibers, while Wetlaid involves a wet process to form a nonwoven fabric. Spunlaid, on the other hand, is a process where continuous filaments are laid down and bonded together using heat and pressure. These different types cater to varied applications in the medical and hygiene sectors.</p></p>
