<p><ul><li>Clear Aligners</li><li>Ceramic Braces</li><li>Lingual Braces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Invisible braces market comprises three main types: clear aligners, ceramic braces, and lingual braces. Clear aligners are made of transparent plastic trays that are custom-fit to the teeth, gradually straightening them over time. Ceramic braces are similar to traditional metal braces but made from tooth-colored ceramic materials that blend in with the teeth. Lingual braces are placed on the backside of the teeth, making them virtually invisible from the front. These options provide individuals seeking orthodontic treatment with discreet and aesthetically pleasing alternatives to conventional metal braces.</p></p>
