<p><p>The Train Transformer Market consists of various types of transformers used in the railway industry. Traction transformers are responsible for converting high voltage power from the grid to the necessary voltage for train operation. Auxiliary transformers provide power for non-propulsion purposes like lighting and air conditioning. Booster transformers increase voltage levels to compensate for power losses during transmission. Isolation transformers are used to separate electrical circuits and ensure safety. These different types of transformers play critical roles in powering and maintaining the functioning of trains.</p></p>
