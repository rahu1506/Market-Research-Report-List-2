<p><p>Wafer Spin Rinse Dryers (SRD) are used in the semiconductor industry to clean and dry silicon wafers after various manufacturing processes. There are two main market types: single stack and double stack SRDs. Single stack SRDs are compact, cost-effective, and suitable for low-volume production. On the other hand, double stack SRDs can handle larger quantities of wafers simultaneously, making them more suitable for high-volume production. Both types perform the same function of rinsing and drying wafers, but their capacity and production capabilities differ.</p></p>
