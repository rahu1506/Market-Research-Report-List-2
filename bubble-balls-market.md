<p><p>Bubble balls are a type of recreational equipment used for various activities. The market for bubble balls can be categorized into two main types: TPU and PVC. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) bubble balls are known for their high durability, flexibility, and resistance to abrasion. They are often preferred for intense and challenging activities. On the other hand, PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) bubble balls are more affordable and suitable for simpler activities. While PVC bubble balls may be less durable than TPU ones, they provide a cost-effective option for casual users.</p></p>
