<p><p>Multi Media Filters are used for the purification of water and wastewater treatment. Mechanical Media Filters involve the physical removal of suspended particles through a filter bed, typically consisting of sand, anthracite, or activated carbon. Chemical Media Filters use activated carbon or other chemical agents to remove dissolved pollutants. Biological Media Filters employ microorganisms to degrade organic matter. There are also other types of media filters, such as granular activated carbon filters, membrane filters, and cartridge filters, which are tailored to specific water treatment needs.</p></p>
