<p><strong>The Automatic Safety Arterial Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FEP</li><li>PUR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Safety Arterial Catheters Market encompasses two types: FEP and PUR. FEP refers to catheters made from fluorinated ethylene propylene, which offers excellent biocompatibility and flexibility. These catheters are suitable for applications such as arterial pressure monitoring and blood gas sampling. On the other hand, PUR stands for polyurethane catheters, known for their durability and kink resistance. These catheters are often used for long-term applications and invasive arterial procedures. Both FEP and PUR catheters ensure patient safety by incorporating automatic safety features, preventing accidental needlesticks and blood exposure.</p></p>
