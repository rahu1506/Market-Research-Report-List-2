<p><p>Oligonucleotide synthesis services market encompasses various types of oligos, namely primers, probes, intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, large-scale synthesis oligos, and linkers and adaptors. Primers are sequence-specific DNA molecules used in DNA amplification processes, while probes are short DNA or RNA sequences that bind to specific target sequences for detection purposes. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos are medium-sized oligos used in research applications, while large-scale synthesis oligos are long DNA sequences employed in therapeutic and diagnostic applications. Linkers and adaptors are short DNA sequences used to connect different oligos or modify their properties.</p></p>
