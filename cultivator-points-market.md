<p><p>Cultivator points are essential components of agricultural equipment used for soil preparation and tillage. Chisel points are long and narrow, designed to loosen compacted soil and provide deep tillage. Triangular points are wider and more versatile, suitable for general tillage and breaking up soil clods. Reversible points have two cutting edges, allowing farmers to work the soil in both forward and reverse directions. These different market types cater to varying soil conditions and farming practices, providing farmers with options to optimize their tillage processes.</p></p>
