<p><strong>The Oil-water Separation Filter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Based</li><li>Chemical Based</li><li>Membrane Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-water separation filter system market is classified into three types: mechanical based, chemical based, and membrane based. Mechanical based systems rely on physical processes such as gravity separation, coalescence, and filtration to separate oil from water. Chemical based systems use chemicals or additives to enhance separation efficiency. Membrane based systems employ a porous membrane that selectively separates oil from water based on molecular size and charge. These different types offer varying levels of efficiency and are utilized in industries such as oil and gas, wastewater treatment, and marine.</p></p>
