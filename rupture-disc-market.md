<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rupture Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Arch Rupture Disc</li><li>Anti-arch Rupture Disc</li><li>Flat Type Rupture Disc</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rupture disc market comprises various types of discs designed for different applications. Positive arch rupture discs are convex discs that provide consistent burst pressures. Anti-arch rupture discs are concave discs designed to prevent the buildup of particles or residues under the disc. Flat type rupture discs are flat or slightly domed and are suitable for applications with lower pressures. Other types in the market include slotted rupture discs, which have slots to control the burst pressure, and reverse-acting rupture discs, which burst in the opposite direction of the process pressure.</p></p>
