<p><p>The glass mosaics market includes two main types: regular glass mosaics and irregular glass mosaics. Regular glass mosaics consist of small, uniform pieces of glass that are arranged in a structured pattern, often forming geometric shapes or repeating patterns. These mosaics offer a clean and organized appearance and are widely used in various applications, including interior design and architectural projects. On the other hand, irregular glass mosaics feature pieces of glass in various shapes and sizes, creating a more artistic and unique look. These mosaics are favored for their creativity and can add a visually appealing element to spaces.</p></p>
