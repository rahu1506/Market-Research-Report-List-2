<p><strong>The PVC Blister Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC blister packaging is commonly used in the market and it comes in two main types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating a PVC sheet and forming it into required shapes using pressure. This type is suitable for packaging products with irregular shapes. On the other hand, cold forming includes the use of aluminum in addition to PVC. This type is preferable for packaging pharmaceutical products as it provides better protection against moisture, light, and oxygen, ensuring product safety and shelf-life. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in the packaging industry.</p></p>
